About Us

Who Are We?

Missouri Lost & Found Paws

Missouri Lost & Found Paws began in August, 2011. We are a social media based organization that is focused on reuniting lost and found pets in the greater St Louis metropolitan area. We reach out to owners of lost pets to help share tips and tricks for getting the word out about lost pets, as well as how to search for lost pets. We also reach out to those who have found lost animals in the area.


We recently became a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. As a non-profit, we are focused on publishing information to local veterinary offices, groomers, and other animal loving establishments. We are a grass roots effort with a simple mission: to get lost and found pets home!
